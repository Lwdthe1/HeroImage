How Do I Use This?

Create Quick Header Image for Your Article

  1. Start by choosing a base image and editing it as desired.
  2. Don't Want to Overlay Another Image?
    • You're done! Click Download.
  3. Want to Overlay Another Image?
    1. Choose an overlay image and edit it as desired.
    2. Click the Merge Images button to overlay your overlay image over your base image.
    3. Choose an opacity for your overlay image.
    4. Scroll down and download the finished merged image.
